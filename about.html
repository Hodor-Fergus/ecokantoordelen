About Us

At ECO KANTOOR DELEN, we are more than just an office space provider—we are a community dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the way people work, we offer eco-friendly, flexible office spaces that cater to the needs of modern professionals and businesses. Whether you're a startup, freelancer, or established company, our spaces are designed to inspire creativity, productivity, and a sense of community. With locations in Antwerp, Hasselt, and Brussels, we are proud to serve a diverse range of clients across these vibrant cities.

Our mission is to create dynamic, sustainable workspaces that empower individuals and businesses to thrive. We are committed to providing high-quality, flexible office solutions that are environmentally responsible and community-focused. By integrating green energy and sustainable practices into our spaces, we aim to reduce our carbon footprint and contribute positively to the environment while offering our clients a comfortable and innovative place to work.

We envision a future where workspaces are not just places to work, but environments that nurture creativity, collaboration, and sustainability. Our goal is to expand our reach, becoming a leading provider of eco-friendly office spaces across Europe, and eventually the world. We aspire to set the standard for sustainable work environments, influencing how businesses operate and how individuals approach their work-life balance.

Sustainability: To continuously improve the sustainability of our office spaces by incorporating more green energy solutions and eco-friendly materials, aiming to be completely carbon neutral by 2030. Community: To foster a vibrant, supportive community within our spaces where professionals can network, collaborate, and grow together. Expansion: To expand our presence in more cities, offering our unique blend of flexible, eco-friendly office spaces to a broader audience. Innovation: To stay ahead of the curve in workspace design and technology, ensuring our clients have access to the most advanced and efficient tools for productivity and collaboration.

At ECO KANTOOR DELEN, we believe that the future of work is green, flexible, and community-driven. We are excited to lead the way in this new era of workspaces.

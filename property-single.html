2550 Kontich, Lannister Building

Kontich, Belgium

Located in the thriving business district of Kontich, the Lannister Building offers state-of-the-art office spaces designed to meet the needs of today’s dynamic companies. This iconic building combines modern architecture with sustainable features, making it an ideal choice for businesses that prioritize both innovation and environmental responsibility.

Choosing the Lannister Building for your office space means aligning your business with sustainability, innovation, and a premium work environment. Our commitment to green energy and eco-friendly practices not only benefits the environment but also enhances your company’s reputation as a forward-thinking organization. With flexible lease terms, cutting-edge amenities, and a prime location in Kontich, the Lannister Building is the perfect place to grow your business and attract top talent.
